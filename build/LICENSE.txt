Valorant Voice Recorder - Automatic Voice Recording for Valorant

This software automatically detects when Valorant is running and records your voice during gameplay. All recordings are saved locally to your Documents/Game Recordings folder.

Features:
- Automatic Valorant detection
- High-quality voice recording (WebM/Opus)
- 2-hour recording limit per session
- Background operation with system tray
- Zero configuration required

By installing this software, you agree to:
- Allow microphone access for voice recording
- Automatic startup with Windows (optional)
- Local storage of recordings on your computer

For support and updates, visit: https://github.com/your-username/valorant-voice-recorder
